## 3. 💭 Self-reflection

**Deepen your improv**
1. How did it feel to have your scene partner (the viewer) dictate your character's traits and actions from off-camera?
2. What strategies did the ensemble use to make transitions between channels seamless and high-energy?

**Beyond the stage**
3. Look back across this course. Which single skill changed the most for you — and where, outside improv, do you most want to keep practising it?
