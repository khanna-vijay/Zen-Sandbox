## 3. 💭 Self-reflection

**Deepen your improv**
1. How did committing 100% to a ridiculous or extreme choice affect your overall confidence in the scene?
2. What did it feel like to actively support someone else's massive, spotlight-stealing moment?

**Beyond the stage**
3. Where in your work or life does an inner editor make you hesitate before you speak? What is one situation this week where you could trust your first, honest thought instead?
