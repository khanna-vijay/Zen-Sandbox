## 3. 💭 Self-reflection

**Deepen your improv**
1. How did it feel to move and vocalize before your brain could label or judge what you were doing?
2. What did you notice about your ability to recover when you felt stuck or lost commitment?

**Beyond the stage**
3. Psychological safety is the container that makes risk possible. Where — on a team, in a family — is the container missing, and what one act would help build it?
