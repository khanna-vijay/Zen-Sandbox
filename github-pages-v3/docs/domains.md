# 🎭 The Five Domains of Improv

Every improv concept lives in one of five domains. Choose one to explore its principles, skills, and techniques (also available in the left sidebar).

## [The Self](content/01_the-self/01_D__the-self.md)

*Freedom from hesitation; complete physical/vocal control; the courage to be truthful and the discipline to be still.*

## [The Partner](content/02_the-partner/02_D__the-partner.md)

*From 'acting with someone' to a 'shared mind' — within a container of mutual safety.*

## [The Scene](content/03_the-scene/03_D__the-scene.md)

*Architect compelling scenes in real time — using both engines of improv and knowing which one the scene wants.*

## [The Ensemble](content/04_the-ensemble/04_D__the-ensemble.md)

*Surrender ego to the piece; perceive, support, generate, and weave — without pre-planning.*

## [The Audience](content/05_the-audience/05_D__the-audience.md)

*Honor the performer–audience contract; read, ride, and unify the room — without pandering.*
