## 2. рџЋІ Today's games

#### Warm-up вЂ” Premise Purge

> Strip away narrative clutter to isolate, commit to, and relentlessly escalate a single comedic game.

![Premise Purge вЂ” infographic](../../games/img/D3_P2_S1_T1_G329__premise-purge-the-focus-enforcer.jpg){ .infographic }

`Players 2+` В· `~15 min` В· `Complexity 3/5` В· `Energy medium` В· `Props: none`

**Trains:** Game Identification В· _skill drill_

**How to play**

1. Select two players to step onto the stage and provide them with a simple, open-ended situational suggestion that establishes a basic environment without dictating a plot.
2. Instruct the players to begin an open scene (the Discovery Phase) for approximately two minutes, focusing on establishing a solid base reality, exploring characters, and letting unusual behaviors or patterns naturally emerge.
3. Once a clear, repeatable pattern or unusual dynamic is established, call out 'PURGE!' to pause the scene.
4. Lead a rapid 30-to-60-second discussion with the players to explicitly define the single most compelling 'game' or pattern that emerged.
5. Direct the players to reset to their starting positions and restart the scene from the very beginning.
6. In this second run, players must immediately establish the base reality and launch directly into the identified game, dedicating every line, reaction, and physical choice to heightening this single premise.
7. Instruct players to actively ignore or reframe any off-game details from the first run, filtering out anything that does not serve the chosen dynamic.
8. If the scene begins to drift, introduce new unrelated conflicts, or lose focus, call out 'TOO WIDE!' to signal the players to immediately pivot their next line back to the core game.
9. End the scene once the game has been heightened to a satisfying peak or fully explored.

#### Core game вЂ” The Pattern Weaver

> Spot the emerging comedic engine, call it out, and jump in to escalate it.

![The Pattern Weaver вЂ” infographic](../../games/img/D3_P0_S1_T1_G485__the-narrative-pattern-weaver.jpg){ .infographic }

`Players 3+` В· `~15 min` В· `Complexity 3/5` В· `Energy medium` В· `Props: none`

**Trains:** Game Identification В· _skill drill_

**How to play**

1. Two players step into the performance space and initiate a standard, relationship-focused scene, establishing a clear platform.
2. The remaining players stand on the sidelines, acting as Weavers who actively listen and watch for emerging behavioral loops, unusual reactions, or repetitive dynamics.
3. As soon as a Weaver identifies a clear pattern or game of the scene, they call out 'Weave!' to freeze the action.
4. The Weaver steps forward and clearly articulates the observed pattern to the players and the room in one concise sentence.
5. The Weaver then tags out one of the active players, taking their place in the scene.
6. The scene immediately unfreezes, and the newly entered Weaver must deliver a line or action that directly heightens, escalates, or explores the specific pattern they just articulated.
7. The scene continues with the new pairing, with the remaining off-stage Weavers continuing to watch for the next evolution or a new pattern to Weave and tag in again.
