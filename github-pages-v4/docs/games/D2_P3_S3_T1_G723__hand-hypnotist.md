# Magnetic Palms

![Magnetic Palms — infographic](img/D2_P3_S3_T1_G723__hand-hypnotist.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Lead and follow through physical connection, keeping your partner safe, successful, and in flow.

## Overview
In this silent physical exercise, players work in pairs where one partner's hand acts as an invisible magnet controlling the movement of the other's face. The follower must maintain a constant distance and angle from the leader's palm as it moves through space, creating a fluid, synchronized dance. The experience builds deep non-verbal connection and shifts the focus entirely onto supporting one's partner.

## What It Trains
- **Domain:** D2 — The Partner
- **Principle(s):** Make Your Partner a Genius; Follow the Follower
- **Skill(s):** Physicality & Space Work; Single-Partner Empathy & Mirroring; Peripheral Awareness
- **Technique(s):** Mirror exercise
- **Focus:** connection

**Objective:** To develop physical empathy, active listening, and peripheral awareness while practicing the core principle of making your partner look brilliant and keeping them safe.

## Setup
An open room cleared of chairs and obstacles. Players stand in pairs facing each other, spaced out comfortably across the floor.

## How to Play
1. Divide the group into pairs and designate one person as Player A (the Leader) and the other as Player B (the Follower).
2. Player A raises one hand, presenting an open palm facing Player B's face at a distance of about twelve inches.
3. Player B locks their focus onto Player A's palm, treating it as a magnetic force that dictates the position of their head.
4. Player A begins to move their hand slowly and smoothly through three-dimensional space—up, down, rotating, and traveling across the room.
5. Player B must move their entire body to keep their face at the exact same distance and angle relative to Player A's palm at all times.
6. Player A must actively protect Player B, using their own eyes to navigate around other pairs and obstacles so Player B never trips or collides.
7. After a few minutes, the facilitator calls out a transition, and the players swap roles so Player B leads and Player A follows.

## Facilitation Notes
- Side-coach the leaders: 'Your goal is to make your partner look graceful and successful, not to challenge or trip them.'
- Side-coach the followers: 'Let go of planning your next step; let your body be pulled effortlessly by the magnet.'
- If leaders move too quickly, remind them that a leader is only as good as their follower's ability to keep up.
- Encourage leaders to use their peripheral vision to monitor the entire room, ensuring their partner's physical safety at all times.

## Variations
- Double Magnet: The leader uses both hands to guide the follower, with one hand controlling the head and the other controlling the follower's hip or shoulder.
- Follow the Follower: Remove the designated leader role; both players put up one hand and negotiate who is leading and following silently and intuitively in real-time.
- The Group Web: Pairs gradually merge with other pairs, creating chains of multiple followers tracking a single leader's hand movements.

## Debrief
- How did it feel to be completely responsible for your partner's physical safety and movement?
- As a follower, what specific movements from your leader made you feel safe enough to take physical risks?
- How does this physical experience of 'making your partner look good' translate to supporting a partner in a spoken improv scene?

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure players are mindful of physical boundaries and comfort levels. If a participant has limited mobility or balance challenges, instruct the leader to keep their hand movements within a comfortable, stable range of motion, avoiding deep bends or rapid travel.

## Why It Works
By removing verbal communication and focusing the leader's attention entirely on the follower's physical success, the game physically embodies the 'make your partner a genius' philosophy. It forces players out of their analytical minds and into a state of somatic empathy and shared flow.
