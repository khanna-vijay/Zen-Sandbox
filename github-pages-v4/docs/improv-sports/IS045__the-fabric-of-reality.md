# The Fabric of Reality

![The Fabric of Reality — infographic](img/IS045.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Players collaboratively construct a narrative that must instantaneously adapt to fundamental shifts in its physical and social rules.

## Overview
Starting with an audience-suggested scene, a referee periodically introduces Reality Shift cards that players must immediately accept and integrate. This forces improvisers to demonstrate extreme Yes, And skills, creative agility, and complete commitment to a continuously evolving, often absurd, reality.

## Setup
The audience provides a single, general suggestion for a scene setting and a basic relationship or objective. Prior to the scene, the referee prepares 3-5 sealed index cards, each containing a single, concise Reality Shift rule (e.g., gravity operates from the side, all objects are sentient, time moves in reverse when standing still) that players are unaware of.

## How to Play
1. The improvisers begin the scene based on the audience suggestion, establishing characters, conflict, and the initial environment.
2. At various points during the scene, the referee dramatically shouts, SHIFT!
3. The referee immediately opens and clearly reads aloud one of the Reality Shift cards.
4. From that exact moment forward, all improvisers must instantly accept this new rule as the absolute truth of their world, physically and verbally adapting to its implications.
5. The scene continues, incorporating the new reality, with subsequent SHIFT! calls introducing new layers of altered reality.
6. The game ends when all cards are played, or the referee calls time.

## Coaching Notes
- Each Reality Shift acts as a sudden plot twist; integrate the new reality into character goals rather than merely reacting.
- Embrace mistakes and unexpected turns; there is no resisting the new truth, so find the narrative opportunity in the absurd.
- Collaborative scene-building is crucial; if one player ignores the shift or tries to revert to the old rules, the scene collapses.
- Commit fully to the physical manifestations of the shifts, such as navigating altered gravity or interacting with sentient objects.
- Pay attention to status play, as shifts can drastically alter who holds power and how relationships function under new constraints.
- Use active listening to quickly grasp how partners are interpreting the new rule to ensure a shared reality.
- Create believable internal justifications for the outlandish new rules.

## Why It Works
It forces improvisers to constantly and fundamentally change their understanding of the scene's environment, applying Yes, And at a macro level to accept the entire rewritten framework of reality. It rewards creative agility and collective commitment to the absurd.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure physical safety when adapting to physical shifts, such as altered gravity or rolling slopes, to avoid collisions or falls. Players should respect physical boundaries and consent, especially when shifts involve physical connection or intense emotional changes.
