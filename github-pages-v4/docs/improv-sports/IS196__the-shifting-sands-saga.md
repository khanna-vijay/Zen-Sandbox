# The Shifting Sands Saga

![The Shifting Sands Saga — infographic](img/IS196.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Players embark on a journey where the very fabric of their reality is constantly unraveling and reforming through sudden, audience-suggested Saga Twists.

## Overview
The Shifting Sands Saga is a continuous narrative game where players embark on an epic journey, but the very fabric of their reality is constantly unraveling and reforming. The Referee periodically introduces sudden, game-changing alterations to individual players' character endowments, the current environment, or immediate scene objectives. Players must quickly and comically 'Yes, And' these unpredictable twists into the ongoing story, proving their adaptability and imaginative prowess.

## Setup
3-4 players on a standard open stage with no props (all object work is mimed). Before the game begins, the audience shouts out short, distinct, and creative suggestions for 'Saga Twists' such as character traits, environmental changes, or new objectives. The Referee jots down 5-7 distinct Saga Twist suggestions on a whiteboard or large cards clearly visible to everyone. The Referee also collects an initial audience suggestion for 'Who are we, where are we, and what is our immediate goal?' to start the scene.

## How to Play
1. The game begins with the players establishing the initial scene based on the audience's 'Who, Where, What' suggestion, focusing on character relationships, environment, and their initial goal.
2. At any point, the Referee claps their hands sharply and loudly declares: 'SAGA TWIST!'
3. Immediately after the call, the Referee points to one player and selects one of the pre-written Saga Twist suggestions from the board.
4. The targeted player must immediately and physically adopt the new twist, integrating it into their character, their interaction with the environment, or their immediate objective.
5. All other players must instantly 'Yes, And' the twist, justifying its sudden appearance within the scene's current reality without questioning or hesitation.
6. The scene continues from this new point, building upon the altered reality as players pursue their objectives.
7. After 30-60 seconds, or when a twist has been fully explored, the Referee calls 'SAGA TWIST!' again, rotating the targeted player or applying an environment change affecting everyone.
8. The game ends after a predetermined time or once all Saga Twist suggestions have been utilized.

## Coaching Notes
- The Rift Foul: Deduct points or call a foul for any player who noticeably hesitates, questions, or fails to fully accept a Saga Twist. This directly punishes a failure to 'Yes, And'.
- Machine Gun Foul: Watch out for one player hogging the scene or dominating the interaction, preventing others from playing out their twists.
- Dynamic Pacing: The Referee controls the pace by calling 'SAGA TWIST!' more or less frequently, allowing for bursts of frantic adaptation followed by periods of comedic development.
- Object Work & Physical Choices: Changing environments and character traits demand strong, inventive physicalization.
- Active Listening: Players must constantly listen and observe for the Referee's call and their partners' reactions to adapt immediately.
- Scoring: Award +3 points for seamless integration of a twist, +2 for inspired 'Yes, And' support from a partner, +1 for narrative flow, and a Team Point for collective creativity. Deduct points for Groaner Fouls (cheap puns) or Rift Fouls (hesitation).

## Variations
- Revisiting a twist from earlier in the scene to see how it layers onto new narrative developments.

## Why It Works
The game demands intense concentration, lightning-fast adaptation, and unwavering commitment. It forces players to absolutely accept every twist and build upon it regardless of absurdity, which heavily promotes 'Yes, And', active listening, and the seamless integration of character endowments and physical choices.

## Safety & Inclusion
Enforce a clean-content foul for any inappropriate or blue humor with an immediate game stoppage and point deduction. Ensure physical choices remain safe during rapid environmental shifts.
