# Mannerism Mayhem!

![Mannerism Mayhem! — infographic](img/IS150.jpg){ .game-hero }

> A fast-paced game of instant adaptation where players swap bizarre quirks on command.

## Overview
Two players from opposing teams perform a scene based on an audience suggestion, each initially assigned a distinct physical, vocal, or emotional 'mannerism.' The core challenge and comedic engine of the game lies in the Referee's unpredictable command of 'TRADE!', at which point players must instantly and seamlessly swap their assigned mannerisms and continue the scene without breaking character.

## Setup
One player from one team and one player from the opposing team step forward onto the stage. The Referee asks the audience for a simple, family-friendly scene suggestion. The Referee then clearly assigns a distinct, quirky, and family-appropriate 'mannerism' to each of the two players (e.g., 'Always takes three steps backward before speaking' or 'Only speaks in questions').

## How to Play
1. The Referee shouts, 'Scene in 3, 2, 1, GO!' and the scene begins, incorporating the audience suggestion.
2. Players must naturally and fully integrate their assigned mannerism into their character's actions and dialogue from the very first moment.
3. After approximately 20-30 seconds, the Referee dramatically shouts, 'TRADE!'
4. Upon the 'TRADE!' command, the two players immediately swap their assigned mannerisms. Player A takes on Player B's mannerism, and Player B takes on Player A's mannerism.
5. Players continue the scene without missing a beat, now embodying their new mannerism with an instant, seamless shift.
6. The Referee calls 'TRADE!' multiple times throughout the scene, keeping the players on their toes.
7. The scene continues for 2-3 minutes, or until the Referee sees a natural ending or calls 'FREEZE!'

## Coaching Notes
- Mannerisms can be physical, vocal, or emotional. Ensure they are clear, distinct, and family-appropriate. They can be from pre-written cards or spontaneous suggestions.
- Call a 'Mannerism Misfire' foul (2 points to the opposing team) if a player consistently forgets, misapplies, or fails to fully commit to their current mannerism.
- Call a 'Slow Swap' foul (2 points to the opposing team) if players are visibly slow, hesitant, or confused during the 'TRADE!' command.
- Call a 'Groaner Foul' (2 points to the opposing team) for exceptionally bad puns or forced jokes, particularly if a vocal mannerism leads to an overtly awful attempt at humor.
- Actively award points throughout the game for Seamless Integration (2 points), Hilarious Execution (2 points), Swift Swaps (1 point per trade to both players), and Yes, And Momentum (1 point).

## Why It Works
The game serves as a challenging exercise in instant character creation and adaptation. The constant 'TRADE!' command demands quick thinking, flexibility, active listening, and the ability to pivot without breaking character, forcing players to accept and transfer endowments rapidly while maintaining scene momentum.

## Safety & Inclusion
The game is inherently family-friendly. The mannerisms themselves must be chosen to avoid anything remotely inappropriate. A clean-content foul is instantly called for any blue humor, swearing, or innuendo, costing the offending player's team 2 points to reinforce the family-friendly ethos.
