# The Time Warp Taxi

![The Time Warp Taxi — infographic](img/IS017.jpg){ .game-hero }

> A bewildered taxi driver and passenger warp through various historical periods, constantly adapting their vehicle, characters, and mission to new realities.

## Overview
The Time Warp Taxi is a high-octane, reality-bending competitive short-form game where a simple taxi ride inexplicably transforms into an epic, hilarious journey across vastly different time periods and locations. A team of improvisers—a bewildered taxi driver and their persistent passenger(s)—must adapt instantaneously to unpredictable temporal shifts, striving to complete an ever-changing, absurd mission. The humor stems from the constant clash between the passenger's unwavering objective and the driver's frantic efforts to navigate history's ever-shifting roads, all orchestrated by a mischievous, time-bending referee.

## Setup
Place two chairs side-by-side at center stage to represent the front seats of the taxi (add a third chair or standing room behind for a second passenger). No physical props are used; all items and the vehicle are mimed. The Referee asks the audience for three initial suggestions: a 'Critical Mission' for the passenger (specific but absurd), a unique 'Quirk/Signature Prop' for the passenger, and the 'First Warp Destination' (a time period and loose location).

## How to Play
1. Initial Scene Establishment (45-60 seconds): The players establish a brief, seemingly normal, contemporary taxi ride. The driver picks up the passenger, establishes the current time/location, and the passenger outlines their 'Critical Mission' and showcases their 'Quirk/Prop'.
2. The 'TIME WARP!' Mechanic: At unpredictable intervals (typically 30-60 seconds), the Referee dramatically shouts 'TIME WARP!' followed by a new time period and a brief, evocative setting.
3. Instant Adaptation: Upon the 'TIME WARP!' call, players must immediately physically and verbally adapt. The 'taxi' morphs through mimed object work, characters adapt their language and status to the new era while retaining their core persona, and the ongoing dialogue and mission must be instantly justified within the new context.
4. 'TEMPORAL DISTORTION!' Challenge: At any point, the Referee can shout 'TEMPORAL DISTORTION!' and introduce a single, contradictory, or anachronistic element that players must immediately integrate and justify into the current warped scene.
5. Call-and-Response: Players should vocally acknowledge the referee's calls (e.g., 'Acknowledged, time lord!') to reinforce the game's energy.
6. Game End: After 5-8 minutes or a predetermined number of warps, the Referee calls 'LAST WARP!' and optionally states a final general destination. Players attempt to bring the ride to a satisfying, coherent, or hilariously ironic conclusion, successfully delivering the fare or achieving their mission.

## Coaching Notes
- The Referee is the 'Time Master' and must dynamically control pacing, escalate complexity with creative warps, and unleash 'Temporal Distortions' to force players to break or stretch established scene rules.
- Award positive points for: Seamless Temporal Transition (+3), Clever Justification & Narrative Link (+2), Warped Witness Wit (+1-2), Strong Character Embodiment/Adaptation (+1), and Paradox Prowess (+3).
- Call fouls and deduct points for: Temporal Confusion (-1 minor, -3 major for freezing or breaking character), Anachronism Agony (bad joke foul, -2 for cheap, uninspired out-of-place jokes), Paradoxical Denial (blocking foul, -1 for negating a teammate's reality), Driving Solo (scene-hogging foul, -1 for dominating the scene), and Temporal Slip (-2 for failing to integrate a Temporal Distortion).
- Encourage strong, specific, and clear mimed object work as the vehicle and environment morph continuously.
- Players must maintain a consistent core character while brilliantly adapting their physicality, voice, and status to wildly different eras.

## Why It Works
This game challenges improvisers to continuously build and then ingeniously break fictional 'rules' within a scene. Guided by a referee who escalates complexity by introducing new, conflicting rules or disruptive elements, it leads to dynamic and surprising comedic payoffs. It acts as a crucible for spontaneous character work, inventive object work, and ingenious narrative justification, demanding high-level 'Yes, And...' skills and rewarding bold choices.

## Safety & Inclusion
Strict adherence to clean-content call rules is paramount to ensure the game remains family-friendly. Any 'blue' humor, sexual innuendo, swearing, or blatant refusal to acknowledge a warp or distortion results in a Major Temporal Confusion foul (-3 points) and is vigorously enforced.
