# Echo Orbit

![Echo Orbit — infographic](img/IS144.jpg){ .game-hero }

> A cooperative game reimagining 'Keepy-Uppy' through imagined sensory properties and non-verbal communication.

## Overview
Players collaboratively sustain an imaginary 'Energy Orb' imbued with distinct sensory characteristics. Each receiver must immediately 'echo' these exact traits through their body and voice, then 'orbit' the orb by transforming just one characteristic before passing it on. This continuous, non-verbal exercise trains sensory embodiment, active listening, and ensemble trust.

## Setup
Participants stand in a loose circle or open space, maintaining awareness of everyone else.

## How to Play
1. One player begins with an imaginary 'Energy Orb' of their own creation, giving it at least two distinct sensory characteristics (e.g., 'a heavy, cold sphere' or 'a light, buzzing cloud').
2. The initiating player physically and audibly embodies holding this orb, clearly demonstrating its characteristics (e.g., straining with weight, shivering with cold, or making a soft buzzing sound).
3. The player 'passes' this orb to another player in the space using a clear physical gesture (e.g., a toss, a gentle push, a levitation, a delicate placement).
4. The receiving player must immediately and demonstrably 'Echo' the orb's current sensory characteristics through their own body and voice.
5. As the receiver 'Echoes' the orb, they subtly or overtly transform exactly one of its characteristics (e.g., 'heavy and cold' becomes 'heavy and warm').
6. The player passing must clearly embody all the new traits as they pass it. They may add a slight verbal cue if helpful (e.g., 'Now it is... spiky!').
7. The game continues with the orb 'orbiting' around the group in a continuous, flowing motion.
8. If a player 'drops' the orb (their echo is unclear, they do not transform a trait, or they seem lost), the game briefly pauses. That player immediately initiates a new Energy Orb with fresh, distinct characteristics to restart the flow.

## Coaching Notes
- Point of Concentration (POC): Receive and physically/audibly echo the exact sensory characteristics of the imaginary orb, then transform only one of those characteristics while embodying all new traits before passing it on.
- Intuition Over Intellect: The swift, continuous flow demands immediate, unthinking physical and vocal responses. Encourage players not to analyze, but to trust their gut reactions to the 'feel' of the orb.
- There is no 'right' or 'wrong' way to embody traits like 'heavy' or 'warm'; vivid physical and vocal commitment is key.
- Remind players that 'drops' are not failures but opportunities to re-engage with renewed focus, fostering a safe space for experimentation.

## Why It Works
It deepens the ability to perceive and physically manifest imaginary sensory details while training players to meticulously observe and respond to subtle physical and vocal cues. It bypasses intellectual planning, forcing instant, intuitive reactions and building ensemble trust through a shared, evolving reality.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure players are mindful of physical boundaries and mobility levels when passing and embodying the orb. Encourage choices that are physically safe and comfortable for all participants to echo.
