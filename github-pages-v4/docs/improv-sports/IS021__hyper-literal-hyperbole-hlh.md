# Hyper-Literal Hyperbole (HLH)

![Hyper-Literal Hyperbole (HLH) — infographic](img/IS021.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Two Athletes perform a mundane task while a Commentator's hyperbolic narration is instantly manifested into physical reality by the Referee's whistle.

## Overview
Hyper-Literal Hyperbole is a fast-paced, high-concept competitive short-form game where two Athletes perform an ostensibly mundane task while a Commentator provides a richly descriptive, often melodramatic, narration. At random intervals, the Referee calls 'HYPER-LITERAL SHIFT!', instantly manifesting the Commentator's most recent hyperbolic, metaphorical, or intensely descriptive statement into the physical reality of the scene. The Athletes must then immediately 'Yes, And' this new, absurd physical reality with utmost commitment, forcing rapid shifts in character, object work, and spatial awareness.

## Setup
Three players are active: two 'Athletes' (Red and Blue) and one 'Commentator.' The Referee oversees the game, calls shifts, and adjudicates scoring. Absolutely no physical props are used; all objects and environmental elements are created and manipulated through precise mime and committed physicality. Use a standard competitive short-form stage configuration. The Referee solicits a single, mundane activity from the audience (e.g., 'making toast', 'filing taxes') to begin.

## How to Play
1. The Referee solicits a single, mundane activity from the audience. Once accepted, the Referee announces, 'Game On, with Hyper-Literal Hyperbole!'
2. The two Athletes immediately begin performing the suggested mundane activity. Simultaneously, the Commentator begins their real-time narration, adding dramatic flair.
3. At any point, when the Commentator uses a particularly evocative, metaphorical, hyperbolic, or intense descriptive phrase that is physically interpretable, the Referee sharply blows the whistle and declares, 'HYPER-LITERAL SHIFT!'
4. The last complete metaphorical or hyperbolic phrase spoken by the Commentator instantaneously becomes a literal, physical reality within the scene.
5. The Athletes must immediately accept this new reality and seamlessly integrate it into their character's actions, physical environment, and emotional state.
6. The Commentator continues narrating the new, shifted reality, accepting it as fact within the game world. Their subsequent descriptions can then lead to further shifts.
7. The Referee awards points: +5 for exceptionally quick and brilliant physical integration by an Athlete, +3 for solid integration, and +3 to the Commentator for engaging narration that sets up strong shifts.
8. The Referee deducts points for fouls: -3 points ('Reality Rupture' Foul) for an Athlete hesitating, denying, or breaking character; -2 points ('Narrative Null' Foul) for the Commentator going 15-20 seconds without literalizable language or pre-emptively engineering a shift; -1 point for standard competitive short-form fouls.

## Coaching Notes
- Prioritize speed and clear physical commitment over perfect execution from the Athletes.
- The spontaneous nature of the Commentator's descriptions is key; they should use rich vocabulary, hyperbole, similes, and metaphors naturally without explicitly explaining or pre-emptively engineering a Shift.
- This game demands a highly intuitive and experienced Referee to identify precise moments and potent phrases for shifts.
- For optimal flow in a typical 3-5 minute game, the Referee should aim for approximately 3-5 significant 'Hyper-Literal Shifts.'
- Watch out for the 'Reality Rupture' foul: Athletes must not hesitate, show confusion, attempt to rationalize or deny a Shift, or break character.

## Variations
- Narrative Freeze: The Referee may, at any emotionally charged moment, call 'Narrative Freeze!' The Commentator falls silent. The Athletes perform a short, dramatic 'slow-motion replay' or highly expressive physical tableau. The Referee then calls 'Narrative Resume!'

## Why It Works
The game generates maximum comedic impact through the collision of the mundane with the magnificently absurd. It develops unparalleled quick wit, physical agility, and absolute 'Yes, And' commitment by forcing instantaneous player adaptation to a continuously evolving, highly physical narrative.

## Safety & Inclusion
Maintain physical safety during rapid physical shifts, mime, and spatial adjustments. Adhere to standard clean-content foul rules: strictly no blue humor, sexual innuendo, or swearing.
