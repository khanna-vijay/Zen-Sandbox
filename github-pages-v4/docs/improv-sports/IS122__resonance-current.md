# Resonance Current

![Resonance Current — infographic](img/IS122.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Players in a circle transmit an invisible, abstract physical quality by deeply embodying it and passing its felt resonance through precise non-verbal gestures.

## Overview
Resonance Current is an improv exercise that adapts simple passing games to train actors in intuitive embodiment and high-fidelity non-verbal communication. Players transmit an invisible, abstract physical quality-a Kinetic Charge-by deeply embodying it and passing its felt resonance through precise non-verbal gestures. The core focus is on perceiving, embodying, and transmitting this sensory quality accurately from player to player without verbalization or intellectual analysis.

## Setup
Participants stand in a circle, facing inwards, at arm's length from their neighbors to ensure enough space for individual physical expression. The facilitator designates one starting player and establishes an atmosphere of non-judgmental discovery. Before starting, lead a brief guided visualization (e.g., imagining holding a piece of ice or a cloud) to prime players to access internal physical sensations and connect directly to bodily experience.

## How to Play
1. The designated starting player inwardly identifies a single, specific, abstract physical quality for an invisible 'Kinetic Charge' (e.g., radiantly warm, brittle and fragile, pulsating and elastic).
2. The player takes a brief moment of internal focus to truly feel this quality within their own body, deeply embodying it as if it were an object in their hands, involving breath, posture, and muscular tension.
3. Once fully resonant, the player physically transfers this Kinetic Charge to the player next to them using a clear, deliberate physical gesture that intuitively conveys the felt quality.
4. The receiving player must intuitively and immediately physically 'listen' and respond to the felt quality, receiving it into their own hands and allowing its embodied quality to spontaneously manifest and resonate throughout their own body.
5. Once the receiving player has clearly embodied the quality and it has 'landed', they become the new giver, re-transmitting that same quality to the next player in the circle.
6. The Kinetic Charge passes around the entire circle, maintaining its essential felt quality and authentic resonance through each transfer and reception.
7. After the Charge has circulated completely, a new quality can be introduced.

## Coaching Notes
- Point of Concentration (POC): To precisely embody, transmit, and receive the unique physical quality of the invisible Kinetic Charge, relying entirely on sensory-motor intuition without intellectual analysis.
- Don't show us 'brittle.' Be brittle. Let your bones feel brittle. Let your breath become brittle.
- Is your transfer clear because you feel the quality so profoundly, or because you are trying to make it obvious?
- When you receive it, let it happen to you. Don't try to do the quality, let the quality do you.
- Did what you just received feel like what your partner was sending? Is the quality still alive when it lands with you?
- Where do you feel that quality in your body right now? Are you making a picture of it, or are you doing it?

## Variations
- Advanced Progression (Dual Charges): Introduce two different Kinetic Charges simultaneously, moving in different directions around the circle.
- Phase 1 (Distinct Reception & Transmission): Player receives two different charges and must transmit each distinctly to different players (e.g., 'brittle' left, 'warm' right).
- Phase 2 (Co-existence): Player receives two different charges, holding and embodying both distinct qualities simultaneously before passing them on.
- Phase 3 (Emergent Qualities): If genuinely felt, players may allow the two qualities to organically create a new, blended quality within them, which they then embody and transmit.
- Vocalizations: Eventually, subtle vocalizations, expressions, or emotional nuances can organically arise from the embodied quality, but the initial focus remains purely physical.

## Why It Works
Players significantly enhance their capacity to deeply attune to and physically manifest subtle non-verbal qualities, fostering somatic intelligence. The game demands rigorous physical listening and precise non-verbal speaking, building sophisticated ensemble communication. Players are continuously challenged to respond authentically in the present moment, fostering profound connection, trust, and mutual reliance within the group.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure players have enough physical space at arm's length to move freely without striking others. Encourage participants to respect their own physical limits and avoid movements that cause pain or discomfort. Maintain a supportive, non-judgmental environment where all physical interpretations are valid.
