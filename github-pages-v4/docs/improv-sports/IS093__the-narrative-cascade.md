# The Narrative Cascade

![The Narrative Cascade — infographic](img/IS093.jpg){ .game-hero }

> A designated 'Cascade Conductor' unilaterally redefines past scene events, revealing a 'new truth' that retroactively re-contextualizes everything.

## Overview
The Narrative Cascade is an improvisational game where a designated 'Cascade Conductor' can unilaterally redefine past scene events at any point. They reveal a 'new truth' that retroactively re-contextualizes everything that has occurred. All players must immediately accept this revelation, collaboratively integrating it into the evolving story, which often results in abrupt genre shifts or profound changes in character motivation.

## Setup
You need 3-4 improvisers. Get an audience suggestion of a mundane object, a simple location, or an opening line to kickstart the initial scene. Designate one player as the 'Cascade Conductor' for a segment of the scene (or the entire scene, by the referee's choice). This player is the only one who can initiate a 'Cascade.' The role can remain fixed or rotate.

## How to Play
1. The scene begins normally, based on the audience suggestion. Players establish characters, relationships, and a foundational premise.
2. At any point during the scene, the designated 'Cascade Conductor' player verbally interjects with a specific trigger phrase, such as 'Wait! I just realized...', 'Ah, but the truth is...', or 'The prophecy foretold...'
3. Immediately after uttering the trigger phrase, the Conductor must provide a piece of information that retroactively re-contextualizes or dramatically changes the meaning of something that has already happened or been said in the scene.
4. The revelation must alter the perceived reality, genre, or core motivations of the scene, explaining previous actions in a completely new light.
5. All players (including the Conductor) must immediately accept this new 'truth' as having always been the case, superseding any previous contradictory 'facts'.
6. Players quickly and collaboratively integrate the new truth into their character's understanding, motivations, and the ongoing plot, building forward from this new reality.
7. The scene continues under the newly established context, exploring the ramifications of the revelation.
8. The 'Cascade Conductor' can initiate multiple cascades throughout the scene, building upon the cumulative, changing narrative. The referee might set a limit (e.g., maximum of 3-4 cascades per scene) to prevent cognitive overload.
9. The scene concludes when a natural dramatic endpoint for the now multi-layered narrative is reached, or when the referee calls time.

## Coaching Notes
- Narrative Development: Each cascade forces a complete re-evaluation of the plot and characters. Demand agile storytelling that not only moves forward but cleverly reinterprets the past.
- Embracing Mistakes: Previous 'established facts' are essentially 'mistakes' that are retroactively justified as part of a deeper, hidden truth. Encourage performers to release attachment to their initial ideas.
- Collaborative Scene-Building: Non-Conductor players must immediately and enthusiastically 'Yes, And' the new reality, helping to make the Conductor's bold move seem brilliant.
- Conductor Responsibility: The Conductor must make a revelation that is playable and inspiring for the others, not just self-serving.
- Justification and Re-incorporation: The Conductor's most crucial skill is to craft a justification that logically explains prior actions within the scene's new context. Other players are then challenged to re-incorporate every element of the prior scene under this new light.
- Status Play: Use cascades to fundamentally alter the status of characters, revealing one to be a secret agent, another a pawn, or their entire world to be an illusion, forcing instant shifts in power dynamics.

## Variations
- Rotating Conductors: Instead of a fixed Cascade Conductor, the role rotates to a new player after each successful Cascade.

## Why It Works
It forces players to practice extreme 'Yes, And' and 'Being Changed' by completely releasing attachment to their initial ideas. By retroactively justifying previous actions as part of a deeper, hidden truth, it fosters a 'no mistakes, only opportunities' mindset and rewards deep, agile narrative development over superficiality.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure that sudden genre shifts or character revelations respect the physical and emotional boundaries of the players. Avoid using the cascade mechanic to force players into uncomfortable or non-consensual scenarios.
