# Pulse Weaver

![Pulse Weaver — infographic](img/IS253.jpg){ .game-hero }

> A non-verbal, intuitive ensemble theater game where participants collectively build and evolve an invisible Pulse through shared, non-cognitive action.

## Overview
Pulse Weaver is a non-verbal, highly intuitive ensemble game designed to create a collective, evolving energy field within a group. Participants continuously sense, respond to, and weave their individual threads into a constantly shifting, collective energetic tapestry.

## Setup
Participants stand in a loose circle or scattered freely in an open space, ensuring everyone can see and sense each other. The facilitator introduces the idea of an invisible, shared Pulse or Energy Field that exists between and among everyone.

## How to Play
1. One participant (or the facilitator) begins by initiating a subtle, non-verbal expression of a specific quality for this Pulse (e.g., a slow sway, rapid flutter, tense breath, humming, or flickering eye movement). This is the first thread.
2. All other players sense this initial thread and intuitively add their own complementary or reactive thread to the Pulse, without intellectualizing, planning, or directly copying.
3. Respond with a physical gesture, non-verbal sound, facial expression, or change in posture that feels congruent with the perceived Pulse.
4. Weave the Pulse collectively and simultaneously. Do not pass it sequentially; everyone continuously contributes to and responds to the emerging group phenomenon.
5. If someone feels the Pulse needs to shift quality (e.g., from tense to relaxed, slow to fast), they initiate that shift through their own non-verbal contribution, and others respond organically.
6. Maintain strictly non-verbal communication throughout the exercise.
7. Continue until the Pulse naturally resolves into stillness, dissipates, reaches a collective climax, or the facilitator calls Freeze.

## Coaching Notes
- Point of Concentration (POC): Intuitively sense and respond to the emergent, collective energy of the group's non-verbal contributions, continuously adding your own authentic, complementary thread.
- Intuition Over Intellect: Demand immediate, sensory, non-cognitive reactions. Overthinking or planning a move will disrupt the flow.
- Everyone Can Act: Remind players there is no right or wrong contribution, only participation.
- Presence & Active Listening: Players must be fully present to sense the Pulse and actively listen via all senses to the group's contributions.
- The facilitator can play a guiding, almost audience-like role, sensing the quality of the pulse and calling Freeze when appropriate.

## Why It Works
It develops intuitive responsiveness and ensemble synchronicity. Players develop hyper-awareness of the group's collective state, learn to contribute spontaneously without intellectual filtering, and build a profound non-verbal connection and shared focus.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure the open space is clear of tripping hazards. Encourage players to move within their own physical limits and respect others' personal space during the collective weaving.
