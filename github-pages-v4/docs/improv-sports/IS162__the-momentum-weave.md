# The Momentum Weave

![The Momentum Weave — infographic](img/IS162.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Players collectively create and sustain an evolving energetic current by passing and adapting physical momentum.

## Overview
The Momentum Weave is a collaborative, non-verbal game where players collectively create and sustain an evolving energetic current. One player initiates a physical action embodying a specific quality of momentum, which is then subtly passed non-verbally to another. The receiver must instinctively embody and uniquely adapt this momentum, allowing it to evolve through their physical response before offering it to the next person.

## Setup
Players stand in a circle or are scattered evenly across an open space. The facilitator explains that the group will collaboratively create and sustain an invisible, evolving 'momentum' or 'energetic current' that flows through them.

## How to Play
1. One player begins by initiating a simple, non-verbal physical action that embodies a specific quality of momentum (e.g., a slow, heavy sway or a rapid, light twitch).
2. This initial action can include a subtle sound (like a hum or breath) if it is intrinsic to the quality of the momentum, but no verbal communication.
3. The initiating player subtly passes this momentum to another player non-verbally through eye contact, a slight lean, or an extended hand gesture.
4. The receiving player immediately and instinctively receives the incoming momentum, embodying it physically and allowing it to move their body.
5. Instead of simply copying the movement, the receiver adapts it, letting the momentum evolve through their unique physical response (e.g., a heavy sway becomes a deep sigh, which then turns into a quick upward gesture).
6. The receiver then passes their newly evolved momentum to another player.
7. The game continues as players receive, embody, adapt, and pass the evolving momentum, aiming to maintain a continuous, unbroken energetic flow until the facilitator ends the exercise.

## Coaching Notes
- Receiver Point of Concentration: Fully feel and allow the incoming momentum to move your body before intellectualizing or deciding on a response. Focus on the physical sensation and instinctive influence.
- Passer Point of Concentration: Keenly observe the potential receiver and subtly, clearly offer the evolved momentum, trusting your ensemble member to respond without pre-determining their reaction.
- Ensemble Point of Concentration: Focus on the continuous, shared, and evolving energetic current flowing through the group.
- Explicitly instruct players to avoid pre-planning their next move or anticipating whose turn it might be. Responses must be immediate and intuitive.
- Discourage verbal language entirely to maintain focus on non-verbal communication and physical intuition.

## Why It Works
It develops the capacity for immediate, authentic physical and energetic response within a group. It sharpens active observation, promotes deep non-verbal communication, cultivates ensemble awareness, and trains players to trust their instincts to build a collective physical intelligence.

## Safety & Inclusion
Players should maintain spatial awareness to avoid collisions. Passing the momentum should be done non-verbally and without physical contact to respect personal boundaries.
