# The Resonance Weave

![The Resonance Weave — infographic](img/ISR136.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Players pass a transforming physical and vocal energy across the room to build deep ensemble focus.

## Overview
A focused ensemble warm-up where players pass a transforming physical and vocal energy across the room. By receiving an impulse, letting it affect their body and voice, and passing a morphed version to someone else, the group builds deep eye contact, active listening, and collective presence.

## Setup
Players stand in a circle or scattered comfortably around the room with enough space to move freely. No props or physical contact are required. The facilitator leads the exercise.

## How to Play
1. The facilitator sets a strict timer for 3 to 5 minutes to keep the energy focused, maintain pacing, and prevent the exercise from dragging.
2. One player initiates the Resonance by making strong eye contact with another player across the room and sending a distinct physical movement paired with a vocal sound. Concrete example: A heavy, slow stomp accompanied by a low, rumbling guggg, or a sharp, quick flick of both wrists with a high-pitched ts-ts!
3. The receiving player must maintain eye contact, catch the energy, and let it physically and vocally land in their body, briefly mirroring or absorbing the impulse.
4. The receiver then transforms the energy, changing its size, pitch, speed, or emotional quality, and passes this new Resonance to a third player using a clear physical and vocal action.
5. The pass must always be readable to the rest of the room. The mandatory vocal and physical components ensure everyone can track the path of the Resonance, keeping the whole group engaged even when they aren't the active sender or receiver.
6. The game continues with the energy weaving across the space, morphing with each pass, until the facilitator calls time.

## Coaching Notes
- Side-coach by saying things like, Make sure we can hear the pass! or Let the energy fully land in your body before you change it!
- Ensure the mandatory vocal and physical components are readable so everyone can track the path of the Resonance.
- Encourage players to bypass intellectual filtering by focusing on immediate, grounded impulses.

## Variations
- Volume Control: The facilitator periodically calls out Amplify or Diminish, prompting the players to make the physical and vocal energy progressively larger and louder, or smaller and quieter, with each successive pass.
- Emotion Weave: Instead of abstract sounds and movements, players pass a specific emotion. The receiver takes in the emotion, lets it affect them, and passes a naturally evolved emotion to the next person (e.g., joy morphs into relief, which morphs into exhaustion).

## Why It Works
It encourages deep ensemble focus, eye contact, and being present while developing physical and vocal transformation skills. It bypasses intellectual filtering by focusing on immediate, grounded impulses.

## Safety & Inclusion
Completely physically and psychologically safe. No physical contact is allowed. Players are encouraged to adapt the physical movements to their own mobility levels; a distinct facial expression or subtle hand gesture paired with a vocalization is just as valid and readable as a full-body movement.
