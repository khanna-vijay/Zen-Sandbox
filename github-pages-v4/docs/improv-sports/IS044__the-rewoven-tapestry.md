# The Rewoven Tapestry

![The Rewoven Tapestry — infographic](img/IS044.jpg){ .game-hero }

> An improv game where a host periodically wipes a specific memory from a character's mind, forcing players to collaboratively re-establish or invent new relationship dynamics.

## Overview
The Rewoven Tapestry is an improvisational game that centers on the dynamic challenge of character memory wipes. Players collaboratively establish initial character relationships and narrative stakes, but periodically, a host designates one character and removes a specific memory or established fact from their mind. The primary objective is for the remaining performers to creatively re-establish or invent new relationship dynamics and scene histories, transforming continuity errors into fertile ground for compelling, non-linear storytelling.

## Setup
Requires a minimum of three players on stage: two primary performers for the scene, and the host who serves as the trigger. Supporting players can also cycle in. Get an audience suggestion for a relationship (e.g., estranged siblings, doctor and patient) and a basic location (e.g., a dusty attic, a spaceship infirmary).

## How to Play
1. The two performers begin a scene establishing their characters, their relationship, and an initial situation or conflict based on the suggestions, quickly building a clear narrative thread and shared history.
2. At set intervals (e.g., every 60-90 seconds, indicated by a bell, buzzer, or the host calling out), or at the host's discretion, the host freezes the scene.
3. The host designates ONE character and announces ONE specific memory, fact, or aspect of their relationship that is now wiped for that character.
4. The designated character loses only that specific memory or fact, not all their memories, while other characters retain their memories.
5. Upon the host's announcement, the scene unfreezes, and the character whose memory was wiped immediately embodies the confusion and lack of that specific knowledge.
6. The other performer(s) must creatively and collaboratively re-establish relationship dynamics and past events in new, creative ways without simply stating 'You did know this.'
7. The other performers use offers, new justifications, emotional responses, and scene shifts to account for their partner's memory lapse, perhaps exploiting the gap for their own character's gain or spinning an entirely new narrative.
8. The memory-wiped character must accept and react to the new information and reinterpretations offered by their partner, continuing to play their newly fragmented reality.
9. This process continues with multiple wipes throughout the scene, building a layer cake of re-negotiated realities.

## Coaching Notes
- Avoid simply repeating information in an attempt to fix the partner; the fix must come from active invention.
- The memory wipe is the mistake or unexpected turn. Performers must not panic but immediately lean into the new reality, treating the loss as a powerful offer.
- When one character loses a memory, the other characters are solely responsible for ensuring the scene doesn't stall. They must actively listen to their partner's confusion and respond with creative offers.
- The memory-wiped character makes their partner look good by fully committing to their confusion and being responsive to the new offers.
- Being Changed: The memory-wiped character is changed, but crucially, the other characters must also be changed by their partner's lack of memory. Their objectives, strategies, and emotional responses shift dramatically.
- Yes, And: Use this not just for agreement, but for building upon the absence of agreement.
- Status Play: The memory wipe can instantly shift status dynamics. The confused character might drop in status, while the remembering character might gain, leading to opportunities for manipulation, guidance, or protection.
- Active Listening & Observation: Crucial for understanding exactly what was forgotten and how to best reintroduce or reinvent that aspect.

## Why It Works
This game directly forces performers to develop narratives non-linearly. Each wipe is a narrative earthquake, demanding a reconstruction that can take the story in surprising new directions rather than merely repeating past information. The art is in the re-exposition: how cleverly players weave in the new truth while honoring the initial premise.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure memory wipes do not target or erase a player's real-life identity, boundaries, or physical safety. Players should maintain physical and emotional check-ins, especially when playing with status shifts and manipulation tactics.
