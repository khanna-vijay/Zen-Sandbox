# The Resonant Current

![The Resonant Current — infographic](img/IS235.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Pass a spontaneous, non-verbal impulse around a circle by responding intuitively rather than imitating.

## Overview
The Resonant Current is a collaborative, non-verbal game where participants stand in a circle and pass a spontaneous impulse around. Instead of imitating, each player receives the impulse, allows it to resonate internally, and then generates their own intuitive sound or gesture as a response. The game fosters deep listening, intuitive responsiveness, and a profound sense of shared presence.

## Setup
Participants stand in a circle, facing inwards, ensuring everyone can clearly see and hear the person on either side.

## How to Play
1. A designated Conductor initiates the game by creating a single, distinct, non-verbal impulse, such as a sound or a physical gesture.
2. The impulse should be spontaneous and originate from an immediate, unbidden feeling or thought, avoiding words, pre-planned melodies, pantomime, or complex actions.
3. The player to the Conductor's immediate left receives this impulse and allows it to resonate within their own body and mind.
4. From this internal resonance, the receiving player spontaneously generates their own single, distinct, non-verbal impulse that feels like the natural continuation of the current, rather than copying the original.
5. This process continues around the circle, with each player receiving from the right and transmitting to the left in a continuous flow with minimal pauses.
6. Absolutely no verbal communication or commentary is allowed, beyond the sounds created as part of the impulses.
7. The game continues for several full rounds, or until the group collectively feels a natural sense of completion or transformation in the current.

## Coaching Notes
- Point of Concentration (POC): Receive the impulse. How does it physically and emotionally resonate within me right now? What unbidden, authentic expression does that resonance demand to be released as my next impulse?
- Encourage players to let go of judgment and simply respond to what they genuinely feel.
- Remind players that the objective is not to get it right or to create a logical sequence.
- Ensure impulses remain simple and avoid pantomime or complex actions.

## Why It Works
It develops intuitive responsiveness by bypassing intellectual analysis, heightens deep listening to subtle non-verbal cues, and cultivates ensemble resonance and connection through a continuously evolving physical and emotional landscape.

## Safety & Inclusion
Maintain a supportive, non-judgmental environment where all authentic expressions are accepted. Players should remain in their own space and avoid physical contact.
