# The Narrative Ripple

![The Narrative Ripple — infographic](img/IS130.jpg){ .game-hero }

> Collaboratively construct a dynamic narrative by seamlessly integrating a series of unexpected, external 'ripples'.

## Overview
The Narrative Ripple is an improvisational game where two or three performers collaboratively construct a dynamic and coherent narrative by seamlessly integrating a series of unexpected, external 'ripples'—sudden changes in circumstance, environment, or character introduced by a host. Performers must immediately justify these new realities, allowing them to genuinely alter the story's direction.

## Setup
You need 2-3 performers, one Host/Judge, and a deck of 'Ripple Cards'. Each card contains a short, evocative instruction or environmental change (e.g., 'The ground shakes violently', 'A strange, metallic scent fills the air', 'One character suddenly develops an inexplicable craving for raw fish'). Get a simple starting suggestion from the audience, such as a location, relationship, or mundane activity.

## How to Play
1. Performers begin a scene based on the audience suggestion, establishing characters, relationship, and initial situation with a clear 'platform' (who, what, where).
2. At regular intervals (e.g., every 45-60 seconds, at a significant beat change, or on a signal), the host draws a Ripple Card and clearly announces its content.
3. Performers must immediately and seamlessly integrate this Ripple into the ongoing scene. It is a new reality, not an optional suggestion.
4. Performers must justify the Ripple's presence, react to its consequences, and allow it to genuinely alter the narrative direction, character relationships, or environment.
5. The scene continues with performers actively 'Yes, And-ing' the new reality and their partners' reactions to it, exploring the implications of the Ripple.
6. The scene ends after a predetermined number of Ripples (e.g., 3-5) or after a set time limit, ideally reaching a natural or dramatically compelling conclusion that incorporates the final Ripple's impact.

## Coaching Notes
- Embrace mistakes and unexpected turns: The Ripples are essentially mandated 'mistakes'. Cheerfully accept these sudden shifts, no matter how illogical they seem, and transform them into opportunities for compelling storytelling.
- Collaborative Scene-Building: Integrating a Ripple successfully requires intense collaboration. One performer might react to the Ripple, and the other must immediately justify, deepen, or expand upon that reaction.
- Being Changed: Performers must allow their characters, relationships, and objectives to be genuinely affected by each Ripple. Sticking to a pre-Ripple agenda will fail.
- Justification: This is the highest skill tested. Performers must invent logical reasons (within the scene's new context) for why these strange Ripples are occurring and how they fit into the evolving story.
- Listening: It is paramount to hear the Ripple clearly and understand how the partner is integrating it.
- Emotional Range & Commitment: React truthfully and fully to the often bizarre Ripples.

## Why It Works
The game inherently forces narrative development. Each Ripple acts as a plot twist, a rising action, or a new conflict, compelling performers to continually re-evaluate and advance the story in unexpected directions. It builds the core muscle of adaptability and flexibility, challenging improvisers to pivot, reframe, and invent on the spot.

## Safety & Inclusion
Ensure physical safety if environmental ripples prompt sudden movements (e.g., 'the ground shakes'). Players should respect physical boundaries and maintain consent when reacting to character or relationship shifts.
