# 🪴 Welcome to the Zen Sandbox

> *Community Guidelines (Online)*

The Zen Sandbox is an online space for presence, collaboration, and radical acceptance. It’s a place to find your *Flow* and play with your *Inner Kid* without fear of judgment. These guidelines keep that space **safe enough to be playful in**. By joining, you agree to uphold them.

!!! abstract "The rule beneath all the rules"
    *Safety is the container play lives inside.* When safety and any other guideline conflict, safety wins.

## The Guidelines

### 1. Practice "Yes, And…" — and honor consent.
Accept what others offer ("Yes"), then build on it ("and…"). We welcome all backgrounds and identities. But "Yes, And" applies to *creative offers, never your boundaries*. You may always decline, redirect, or step out, no explanation needed.

### 2. Anyone can call "Cut".
At any moment you may stop a scene by typing or saying **"Cut."** No explanation required, no one questioned. You can also turn off your camera/mic or leave at any time. Respect a boundary the instant it's set.

### 3. Make your partner look good.
Improv is a team sport. Listen deeply, build on others, and set partners up to shine. Generosity over ego, don't talk over people or hog the focus (especially tricky on a call).

### 4. Honor the Beginner's Mind.
We practice *Shoshin*: showing up open. This takes courage, so we protect it. **Do not mock, shame, or pass personal judgment** on anyone's contribution. There are no mistakes, only unexpected gifts.

*Feedback is how we grow.* So we keep it safe: give notes **only when invited, and only about the work, never the person.** Ask first ("Want a note?"), keep it specific and kind, and accept "no thanks" gracefully.

### 5. Leave the heavy bags at the door.
We keep partisan political/religious debate, proselytizing, and divisive current-events arguments out of scenes and chat.

### 6. Keep it PG.
Keep language, themes, and scenes roughly **PG / family-friendly** in voice, in video, and in text chat. No explicit content, graphic violence, slurs, or heavy swearing. When in doubt, lighten it. Always assume someone’s kids/grand-parents could be walking-by other online participants, and we need to leave a good impression.

### 7. Zero tolerance for harm.
Separate from any craft note: **harassment, hate speech, discrimination, threats, bullying, or personal attacks in scenes, chat, DMs, or usernames/avatars may result in immediate removal.** This is a condition of membership, not an improv choice.

### 8. What happens in the sandbox stays in the sandbox.
Vulnerability needs privacy. **Do not record, screenshot, or share** others' words, faces, or personal stories outside the group without explicit permission.

!!! note "Exception"
    Confidentiality never stops you reporting harassment or safety concerns to organizers or authorities if someone is in danger.

## 🆘 If something goes wrong

**Report to:** [name/role] via [DM / email / "Report" channel]. Handled **confidentially** and promptly.

**Our response, generally:** ① friendly reminder → ② formal warning → ③ removal from a session or the community for severe or repeated violations.

**Need a moment?** You can step away, mute, or DM a moderator privately at any time.

**Scope:** Applies across **all our online spaces** — live sessions, breakout rooms, group chat, DMs between members, and community events.
